Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FSWI

How can I become a member of FSWI?

To become a member of FSWI, you can contact us through our website or email us at fswiinfo@gmail.com. We welcome all women in the fire service, including firefighters, paramedics, dispatchers, and other fire service professionals.

What events does FSWI host throughout the year?

FSWI hosts various events including training sessions, networking opportunities, conferences, and community outreach programs. Check our social media pages and contact us for upcoming event information.

How can I get involved with FSWI activities?

There are many ways to get involved! You can volunteer for events, join committees, participate in training programs, or help with community outreach. Contact us to learn about current opportunities.

Does FSWI offer training or certification programs?

Yes, FSWI offers various training programs and professional development opportunities. We work with certified instructors and organizations to provide quality training for our members.

How can I support FSWI's mission?

You can support FSWI by becoming a member, attending events, volunteering your time, making a donation, or spreading awareness about our organization and mission in your community.
